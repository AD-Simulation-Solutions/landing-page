Solutions for Autonomous Driving

Natural Scenarios

Real-world driving behavior captured directly from urban surveillance cameras. Collected traffic data from intersections, highways, and complex road nodes is reconstructed in precise 2D/3D road coordinates.

Scenarios expose autonomous systems to naturalistic traffic patterns, rare events, and unpredictable human behavior not found in synthetic schemas. This allows reproduction of highly realistic driving scenarios by any ADAS/AD simulator.

Classified Data Sets

A structured, searchable library of real-world driving scenarios with full categorization. Datasets include thousands of episodes sorted by:

  • Participant type categories
  • Maneuver complexity
  • Collision risk levels
  • Rare-event occurrences
  • Traffic density

Enriched with metadata for AD/ADAS training & validation.

Scenario Recording & Reconstruction

Surveillance-Based Capture

We capture large-scale real-world traffic using CCTV systems installed at roads and intersections. All traffic participants are tracked in real-time and mapped to an accurate land-coordinate system.

Trajectory-Based Reproduction

From reconstructed motion data, we generate simulation-ready scenarios where the ego-vehicle can be placed in the position of any participant. This enables exhaustive testing in realistic conditions.
