The Problem

Synthetic data alone isn't enough

Purely generated scenarios miss the chaotic, unpredictable reality of actual traffic. Your models need to learn from what humans really do — not what simulators think they do.

✕ Fully Synthetic Scenarios

  • Scenarios follow programmed rules — real drivers don't
  • Combinatorial explosion: billions of permutations, most unrealistic
  • Near-miss events and edge cases are guessed, not observed
  • Months of compute to generate exhaustive test suites
  • Sim-to-real gap causes field failures

✓ ADSS Real-World Scenarios

  • Captured from actual traffic at complex intersections
  • Pre-filtered to high-value situations — no wasted compute
  • Real near-misses, violations, and edge cases included
  • Continuously growing from 850+ live camera feeds
  • Proven sim-to-real alignment — test what matters
